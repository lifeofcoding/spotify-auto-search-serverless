Log in with Spotify

Load any existing search query

Text box for input (line separated)

Save button --> saves you search qur against your account

Search button

------------------------------
Results separated by search query, shows top 3 results per search

Option to click on a search result to add it to your library, or to one of your playlists
Tickbox to remove it from your search query


-----------------------------
Option to automatically search for songs on a weekly basis, and email you results
Email contains link with a UUID, links to search results with above options to save etc (will require a sign in)
Link expires after next search executed
